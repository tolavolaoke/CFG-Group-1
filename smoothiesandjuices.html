How about try and make your own!
To make you own type of smoothie follow this simple guide:


1. Add a fruit
2. Choose a base e.g milk, fruit juice, iced coffee, iced green tea, coconut water milk, water
3. Thicken it up e.g yoghurt, ice cubes, oats/beans, chia seeds, pea nut butter
4. Flavour it e.g honey, cinnamon, dates, herbs
5. Power boost e.g spirinila, vitamin powder
