Weekly Workout Plan

We have arranged a weekly workout to help you get started!

MONDAY

Butts, Legs and Thighs Day:

30 Squats
15 Lunges (each leg)
30 Donkey Kicks (each leg)
Repeat 3x

TUSEDAY

Abs Day:

30 Sit Ups
25 Bicycle Crunches (each side)
15 Scissor Kicks (2 kicks count as 1)
Repeat 3x

WEDNESDAY

Arms and Back Day:

10 Push Ups
15 Supermans
10 Diamond Push Ups
Repeat 3x

THURSDAY

Full Body Day:

10 Burpees
15 Mountain Climbers
12 Jumping Squats
Repeat 3x

FRIDAY

Abs Day:

10 V-Ups
15 Reverse Crunches
45sec Plank
Repeat 3x

SATURDAY

Full Body Day:

25 Plie Squats
15 Curtesy Lunges
30 Hip Thrusts
Repeat 3x

SUNDAY

Arms Day:

10 Push Ups
15 Chair Dips
8 Diamond Push Ups
Repeat 3x
